# **App Name**: Neup.Suite

## Core Features:

- Company Onboarding: Seamlessly guides clients through company registration, KYC, document submission, compliance checks, and contract signing with automated follow-ups and reminders.
- Client Dashboard: Gives each client a personalized hub showing live project progress, assigned tasks, payment status, key contacts, upcoming milestones, and performance metrics.
- Communication Hub: Centralizes all client interactions with in-app chat, threaded discussions, file sharing, tagging, and advanced search to keep every conversation organized and retrievable.
- Billing & Invoicing: Handles invoice generation, automated billing cycles, integrated payment gateways, tax-compliant receipts, and historical billing records.
- AI “What’s Next” Assistant: Tool Acts like a project guide for clients, highlighting pending actions, upcoming deadlines, and suggesting proactive steps based on real-time project data.
- Knowledge Base AI Chatbot: Provides an AI-driven Q&A bot trained on your KB that answers client FAQs, explains processes, and provides instant support based on your internal documentation and SOPs.
- Smart Alerts & Notifications: Automated push/email alerts for key events—missed deadlines, contract renewals, new updates—so clients never miss a beat.
- Role-Based Access Control: Separate internal teams, client teams, and external collaborators with fine-grained permissions. Shared spaces allow transparent work while protecting sensitive internal data.
- Task & Project Tracking: Assign, monitor, and manage tasks with timelines, Gantt charts, and dependencies, visible both to internal teams and clients.
- File & Document Repository: Version-controlled storage for contracts, reports, assets, and legal docs with tagging, expiry alerts, and approval flows.
- Client CRM Module: Manage client profiles, contact persons, historical interactions, deals, and internal notes in one clean CRM-like interface.
- Proposal & Contract Builder: Create, send, and get digital signatures on proposals and agreements with pre-built templates.
- Feedback & Satisfaction Surveys: Automated client check-ins and CSAT/NPS tracking to monitor satisfaction and identify upsell opportunities.
- App Integrations & API: Allow clients to connect their systems (e.g., Slack, Google Drive, Notion, CRM) or use a clean API for custom integrations.
- Client Learning Hub: Self-serve training videos, courses, and SOP guides to onboard client teams faster.
- Affiliate & Partner Portal: Manage partner companies or referral clients with dedicated dashboards and tracking.
- White-Labeling: Let premium clients use their branding within the portal for a personalized experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: HSL(210, 70%, 50%) - A vibrant blue (#3399FF) to convey trust, professionalism, and forward-thinking.
- Background color: HSL(210, 20%, 95%) - A light, desaturated blue (#F0F8FF) for a clean, airy feel that is easy on the eyes.
- Accent color: HSL(180, 60%, 40%) - A contrasting teal (#33A6A6) used to highlight important CTAs and interactive elements.
- Body text font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif suitable for body text.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a techy sans-serif for headers.
- Clean, modern icons that represent various services and features.
- A clean and organized layout with clear section headings and intuitive navigation.
